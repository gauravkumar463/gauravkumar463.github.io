My Research

One of my current research interest is developing a predictive adaptive mesh refinement technique which can identify the grid points for successive refinement at the next time step (not present time step as done in all the CFD solvers with AMR) improving the efficiency and accuracy of the simulation. This technique is also fully automated to resolve maximum physical flow interactions and require no arbitrary user input. My broader research interests include high-speed flows, aeroacoustics and acceleration of general multiphysics simulations.
